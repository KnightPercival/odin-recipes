Low Calorie Pizza Dip

Want to lose weight but love pizza cant live without it here is a low calorie pizza dupe all the joy of eating pizza but no guilt after maybe even seconds.

Ingredients

  • Cottage Cheese recommendation (Good Culture Cottage Cheese) 1 serving.
  • Marinara Sauce recommendation (Rao's HomemadeÂ® Marinara Sauce) 60 grams or half a serving.
  • Italian Seasoning to your liking.
  • Garlic Powder just to your liking.
  • Mozzarella Cheese recommendation (Kraft Fat Free Mozzarella Shredded Natural Cheese 7oz Bag) 1 serving.
  • Turkey Pepperoni recommendation (HORMEL, Turkey Pepperoni, Pizza Topping) 1 serving.
  • Low Cal Tortilla recommendation (Mission Carb Balance Taco Size Soft flour Tortillas) 1 serving.

Steps

  1. Mix the Cottage Cheese and Marinara Sauce.
  2. Mix in the Italian Seasoning and Garlic Powder into the mix.
  3. Layer on top the Mozzarella Cheese then the Turkey Pepperonis.
  4. Cut the Low Cal Tortilla into Chip slices.
  5. Microwave the Tortilla for 1 minute to 2 minute depending on how crispy you want your chips.
  6. Finally Microwave the Pizza Dip for 1 minute and 30 seconds and enjoy.
