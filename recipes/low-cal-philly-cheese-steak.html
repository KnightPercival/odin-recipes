Low Cal Philly Cheese steak

Philly Cheese Steak so good you won't believe its so low calorie eat 1 or 2 severing's at half the calories of a regular one.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef recommendation (Any 93% lean Ground Beef) 1lb.
  • Sweet Green Bell Pepper 142g.
  • Sweet Yellow Onion 85g.
  • Au Jus Gravy Mix 6tsp.
  • Provolone Cheese recommendation (Sargento Provolone Natural Cheese Ultra Thin Slices) 2 Slices.
  • Low Calorie Hotdog Buns recommendation (Old Tyme 647 Long Roll (Hot Dog Bun)) 1 Bun

Steps

  1. Season Ground Beef in pan with SPG and Au Jus Gravy Mix
  2. Add Sliced Yellow Onion and Sliced Green Pepper.
  3. Cook till Ground Beef is browned and vegetables are softened.
  4. Tost Hotdog Buns.
  5. Load Hotdog Buns with meat and layer on top Provolone Cheese and Enjoy.
