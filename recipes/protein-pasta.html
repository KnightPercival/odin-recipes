Protein Pasta

Want to lose weight and love big portions Protein Pasta is for you high volume low calorie food eat to your fill at half calories of your moms spaghetti without having to sacrifices the taste.

Ingredients

  • Past recommendation (Barilla Protein+) any type of pasta Rotini, Penne ect. 1 box.
  • Ground Turkey (99%) lean recommendation (Any 99% Fat Free Ground Turkey) 1.3lb.
  • Taco Seasoning recommendation (Taco Bell Seasoning Original) 1 packet.
  • Corn 15oz can.
  • Rotel 10oz can.

Steps

  1. Mix the Cottage Cheese and Marinara Sauce.
  2. Mix in the Italian Seasoning and Garlic Powder into the mix.
  3. Layer on top the Mozzarella Cheese then the Turkey Pepperonis.
  4. Cut the Low Cal Tortilla into Chip slices.
  5. Microwave the Tortilla for 1 minute to 2 minute depending on how crispy you want your chips.
  6. Finally Microwave the Pizza Dip for 1 minute and 30 seconds and enjoy.
