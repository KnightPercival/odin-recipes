Protein Taco Pasta

Want to lose weight and love big portions Protein Pasta is for you high volume low calorie food eat to your fill at half calories of your moms spaghetti without having to sacrifices the taste.

Ingredients

  • Past recommendation (Barilla Protein+) any type of pasta Rotini, Penne ect. 1 box.
  • Ground Turkey (99%) lean recommendation (Any 99% Fat Free Ground Turkey) 1.3lb.
  • Taco Seasoning recommendation (Taco Bell Seasoning Original) 1 packet.
  • Corn 15oz can.
  • Rotel 10oz can.

Steps

  1. Follow instructions on the pasta box.
  2. While the pasta is cooking start cooking the Ground Turkey till it's a nice brown.
  3. Once the Ground Turkey is brown add the Corn and Rotel mix well.
  4. Pasta should be done by now drain the water save 1 cup add it to the Turkey along with the Taco Seasoning and Pasta.
  5. Mix well and enjoy.
